# Code of Conduct

This project follows a practical maintainer standard:

- Be respectful and specific.
- Use synthetic examples only.
- Do not post customer data, credentials, private business records, or production documents.
- Assume contributors may work across English, Chinese, and Thai language contexts.
- Keep disagreements focused on workflow correctness, security, and maintainability.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that risks exposing private information.
